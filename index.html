Fun Facts About Smarties

  • Smarties were first produced in 1937 by Rowntree & Company Limited in the UK.
  • In the United States, Smarties refer to a popular sugar-coated tablet candy, while in the UK, the term is associated with a different chocolate confectionery.
  • Smarties are known for their vibrant and assorted colors, making them visually appealing.
  • The candy comes in a roll, and each piece is individually wrapped, making it convenient for sharing and portion control.
  • Smarties are popular not only for their taste but also for the nostalgic and colorful experience they provide.
